Elections

Our democracy is already facing the type of existential threats we have never seen in generations before ours and the beginning of the solution to these numerous problems is in drafting a new constitution entirely to replace this current failed one in operation for 22 years.

Nigeria’s single problem has been constitutional and unless replaced, things will only continue to get even worse because the constitutional journey of any democracy never ends, but occasionally pauses for a re-constitution; if the existing constitution starts to fail.

We strongly believe it’s a no-brainer to keep using the same failed socialist constitutional model to run our country to the ground but other youths have shown that they think otherwise because millions are still on social media blindly campaigning for the Nigeria Plc Establishment, just to re-validate the continuation of the operations of the rotten 1999 constitutional governance.

So, RAP has taken it as a civic responsibility to recruit a fresh hands of 476 patriotic youths across all constituencies in Nigeria with a clear purpose to save Nigeria this 2023 by re-constituting a New Nigeria Republic through the exercises of constituent power to redraft a new constitution we choose to name, the bottom-line.

Consequently, if you (or someone you know) have been saying for years “I could do so much better than those old idiots who get selected” please, Nigeria is looking for you.

Nigeria Youths born between 1975 - 1995 to please join the democratic evolution and help build a New Nigeria Republic.

We shall be selecting party members across Nigeria to run for the 112 Senatorial Seats and 364 Representatives Seats across Nigeria, which will include Abazonians and the people of Bacasi Peninsula with single Senatorial Seats and two Representatives Seats each.

We have heard the cry of those marginalized and as a Libertarian Party, we are devoted to the ideals and practices of democracy legitimately, and our focus is removing the 1999 constitutional regime in this coming election.

Our strategy is to cliche NASS this coming general elections and change everything because there’s no transparency in the executive, or accountability at the legislative arm, nor any public services delivered to citizens both in states and local government.

Join our democratic evolution because now is the right time to find some friends who are ambitious go-getters to run for the legislative arm of our government. Together armed with the bottom line constitution and the rule of law, we can renew our democracy and protect it from Old Nigeria Plc politicians who are already doing it harm.

Because when a constitution like the 1999 fails as it has for 22 years. It can be rebuilt by the next generation on a much stronger foundation. That’s why our sole mission for this 2023 general election as a political party is to rebuild a New Nigeria Republic that will give everyone a fair shot.
