Who We Are

The RAP, also referred to as the golden lions of Africa, is the only African party that has undergone an ideological shift from left to right to oppose this continuous expansion of neocolonialism in Africa.

The Republican African Party in acronyms like RAP, traces its roots down to Herbert Macaulay and the rise of the Pan Africanist youth movement in the 1900’s as a reaction against the domination of old political parties by aging political elites who have all drifted into socialism - neocolonialism. ...

Neocolonialism is a 20th century systematic practice by the G7 nations using economic imperialism, globalization and international organizations' policies to indirectly maintain their influence over Africa, the continent, the British West Indies and the Caribbean Islands.

This concept underscores how African state's sovereignties have been reduced to a “mere flag of independence” by their external policy interference and economic control.

Consequently, a libertarian party was formed in 2015 in Lagos as an ideological principle-based political party to bring people of African decent worldwide to fight neocolonialism together in our continent and the Caribbean islands. This party is solely created for African Youths and it is not a means to grab power like others, but to provide a way for citizens to participate in their government constructively.

Basically, our philosophy centers on citizenship in a state-organized republic and our general beliefs are self - reliance, faith, tradition, nationalism, low taxes, freedoms, retribution and a free market economy.
