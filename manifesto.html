Manifesto

Dear Fellow Nigerian Youths, POLITICAL PROGRAMS TO BUILD A BRAND NEW NIGERIA Now is our time to bind the wounds of division, youths have to get together. To all Igbo youths, Yorubas, Hausas, Fulanis, Ijaws and Kanuri youths across the nation. We say it’s time to come together as one united generation because we have been sitting down for so long looking at our country degenerate by failed old generational establishment leadership. For those who have chosen to continue to support the Nigeria Plc establishment for this past 24 years of their kakistocracy, we are reaching out to you for your guidance and your help to build a New Nigeria Republic, unifying and saving our great country from failing. The forgotten men and women of our great country will be forgotten no longer. Your voice, your hopes and your dreams will define our country destiny and your courage, goodness and love will forever guide us along the way to our freedom from this neocolonialist regime. You have voted PDP or APC before, and it’s the-same failed outcome they have always delivered. So what do you have to loose, if you support us to help reclaim our country’s destiny and dream big and bold and daring because as a young Nigerian you basically don’t have anything to loose again. We have to do this together! You all have given us such an incredible support and our work on this movement is now really just beginning. Our primary goal is to build a New Nigeria Republic founded on equity and justice. Consequently, we have a plan for a brand new constitution that would be called the bottomline constitution. It would kickstart the New Nigeria Republic. You may feel the die is cast and that there is no way to choose an alternative path any longer. But a New Nigeria is always possible because the future is not written until we write it ourselves. And no, it is not too late to change the way all things are currently going. But to do so, we must do so with conviction and conscious effort that we want better and not the same failed crop of political parties because our nation is now a youths canvas and the New Nigeria Project is our masterpiece. On 12th June, 2023 we will declare a New Nigeria Republic together by replacing the current corrupt 1999 Marxist Military Socialist Constitutions with the original constitutional script of the USA founding fathers as our base foundation to formulate the Bottomline Constitution that will radically reduce the size, scope and reach of the Federal Government. Thus the future is now Nigerians & our manifesto is as follows:- a. SOCIO - ECONOMIC & POLITICAL REALIGNMENT - Granting Full Citizenships to all who believed in our New Nigeria Republic & an e-residency for all our brothers in diaspora - Free Speech, censorship, health mandates & all forms emergency power - Youths as leaders & gender parity for all - Official 4days working week (Mon - Thu) - New Nigeria Healthcare Policy NNHP Bill - On 20th May, 2023 declaration of the legalization & commercialization of marijuana Bill - Reformation of Nigeria Criminal Justice System & judiciary independence - Innovative free market economy with low taxes & tariffs - 6 provincial states - Financial Services Reformation to expand our economic scope - New Nigeria Affordable Housing Policy NNAHP Bill b. NATIONAL SECURITY & DEFENSE - Personal Security - Job security & food processing technology investment - Redrafting a New Nigeria Defense Policy - Recalibrate the law enforcement to fit in the New Nigeria structure & system - life insurance policy for everyone in the New Nigeria Armed Forces with global standard welfare package plus scholarships c. MODERN EDUCATION & SPORTS TECHNIQUES -Remodeling of our educational curricula & System -Literacy ( English, French & Pan African History) with the 6 provincial state mandating an indigenous language to preserve it -Science & Technology -Sports & Arts/ Crafting -Youth Empowerments (NYSC) only mandatory for all 18 years old citizens educated or not, to assess & help their development d. ENERGY INDEPENDENCE - Electricity - Oil & Gas - Coal Power - Renewables & removal of all their tariffs - Atomic f. FOREIGN AFFAIRS - Citizenships focus & ECOWAS based plus New Nigeria Citizens strong globally - Geo - Political Regional Power in Africa & the world - Renegotiate all old Nigeria Republic sovereigns international debts & all there international associations agreements - Any Citizens of the New Nigeria will only pay a visa fee for visas granted g. INFRASTRUCTURE UPGRADES - National Broad Band 5G Network - Rebuilding Agricultural Infrastructures & Restructure Transportation Supply network in ECOWAS - International mega Airport Hubs Strategically located in each part of the 6 provincial states - Standard sea ports for each coastal provincial states and strategic dry port for inter land states h. ELECTORAL REFORMS - Decentralization of INEC - Make Election Day a holiday - Massively expand voting access through automated national voters registration & single verification standard - Paper Ballots & no digital voting for recertifications of results purposes - Requiring The Provincial States 5 days early voting - Political Parties will only be active at the Federal & Provincial levels - Only Citizens, Residents, Business or associations in New Nigeria may contribute to political parties - Enacting an anti - election interference policy against foreign governments interference i. ECOWAS - Revival of Economic Security Blocs ECOMOG - Activation of ECO common currency - Common Energy Policy - Common Education Policy Give us just one try, we will fight the special interest for you with every breath in our body and we will never ever let you down.
